Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? We’ve Got Answers

When did Dave Jethro start his music career?
Dave started his professional music journey in the early 2020s, blending R&B/Soul and gospel to create a unique and heartfelt sound.
What are some of Dave’s most popular songs?
Some of his notable songs include GLO (2024), Real Love (2025), and HIGHER (2025), which showcase his powerful vocals and inspirational message.
Does Dave Jethro perform live concerts and tours?
Yes! Dave regularly performs at concerts, festivals, and church events across Uganda and internationally. Check the Insight page for upcoming events.
Can fans book Dave Jethro for private events?
Absolutely. Fans and organizers can book Dave for weddings, concerts, and special events. Reach out through the Contact page for booking information.
Is Dave Jethro releasing an album soon?
Yes, Dave is currently working on a full studio album expected in 2026. Stay tuned for official announcements on his social media and website.
Where can I buy Dave Jethro merchandise?
Merchandise is available on the Merchandise page, including t-shirts, hoodies, and other fan gear.
How can I join the friends community?
Fans can join the community by following Dave on social media, sharing reviews on the Friends page, and subscribing to the newsletter.
