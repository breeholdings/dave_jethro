About

Dave Jethro crafts heartfelt worship songs that blend intimate lyricism with warm, melodic arrangements. His music is rooted in faith, aimed at leading listeners into reflection and joyful praise.

Artist Statement

My music is an offering — songs written to carry hope, encourage worship and create space for encounter. I write from life, scripture, and the moments God meets us in the ordinary.
