About Aueral Band

The Aueral Band, led by Dave Jethro, is a collective of passionate musicians who bring live music to life. From soulful singers to skilled drummers, pianists, guitarists, and more — we perform at weddings, concerts, corporate events, and private parties, creating unforgettable musical moments for every occasion.
