Inside the Music

Stories, Sounds & Moments That Define Dave Jethro

Beyond the Studio

Dave Jethro’s sound continues to evolve with every release — fusing gospel soul, R&B warmth, and heartfelt storytelling. From intimate worship ballads to genre-bending collaborations, his journey reflects authenticity, vulnerability, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

2AM — The Album

New Album Coming Soon

2AM is Dave Jethro’s most personal and emotionally charged project yet. Crafted during quiet midnight sessions, the album dives into themes of love, restlessness, and late-night introspection. From stripped-down ballads to ambient R&B soundscapes, 2AM captures the emotions that surface when the world sleeps but the heart stays awake.

Musical Evolution

From the early acoustic sessions that birthed “Grace Notes” to the powerful emotional resonance of 2AM, Dave’s music tells a story of transformation. His sound has expanded to embrace live instrumentation, deeper lyricism, and bold collaborations that cross musical boundaries.

Notable Singles & Collaborations

“Grace Notes”

The breakout single that defined Dave’s soulful tone — a heartfelt reflection of faith and emotion.

“Hold You Still” ft. Bree

An R&B collaboration blending vulnerability with silky harmonies and emotional honesty.

“Golden Hours”

A cinematic love song released ahead of the 2AM album — intimate, tender, and timeless.

“Echoes” ft. Bree

Experimental yet spiritual, merging spoken word with ambient soul for a haunting experience.
